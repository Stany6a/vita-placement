How we work

Finding the right care for a loved one is one of life's most emotional and complex decisions. At Vita, we believe that life should be lived with dignity, comfort and joy at every stage. We act as your personal navigators in the world of senior living.

01.

Personal assessment

We take the time to understand your loved one's unique needs, preferences, budget, and care requirements before exploring options.

02.

Expert matching

Using our in-depth knowledge of local Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Memory Care communities, we create a tailored shortlist of recommended options.

03.

Guided tours

We arrange and accompany community tours, helping you evaluate each option and ask the questions that matter most.

04.

Seamless transition

Once you've made a decision, we help coordinate the next steps and guide you through the move-in process.
